Account and data deletion requests

If you want your cloud-synced account data deleted, please contact us by email and include the account details needed to confirm ownership. For security reasons, we may ask you to verify account ownership before processing the request.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple. You can manage, restore, or cancel subscriptions in your App Store account settings. If your subscription status looks incorrect, contact us and we will help investigate.
