AI Wealth Ledger Privacy Policy

This page explains what information AI Wealth Ledger processes, how it is used, which third-party services are involved, and what choices you have.

Updated: April 15, 2026 Effective: April 15, 2026 Product: AI Wealth Ledger

1. Information the app processes

1.1 Information you enter or create

  • Bookkeeping entries: amount, type, category, date, notes, and related transaction details.
  • Asset data: account name, balance, asset type, and related asset records.
  • Stock positions: ticker symbol, quantity, cost basis, quote snapshots, and related portfolio data.
  • Custom categories: categories you create in the app.
  • Receipt images: images you choose to upload for OCR processing.
  • Text and voice-derived input: text you enter or speech recognition results you submit for AI parsing.

1.2 Device-side and local app data

  • Local preferences: locale, country, base currency, consent flags, cached UI state, and similar local settings.
  • Search and quote cache: cached stock results and quote refresh timestamps used to improve performance.
  • Diagnostics: limited logs and transient error information used for troubleshooting.

1.3 Sign-in information

If you use the international sign-in flow, the app may process your email address, display name, provider identifiers, or local sign-in status as needed to keep you signed in and synchronize your account data.

We do not proactively upload receipt images, AI input text, or cloud-sync data unless you explicitly use the related feature.

3. How the app uses your information

  • To let you create, edit, organize, and review bookkeeping and asset records.
  • To synchronize your account data across sessions and devices when you sign in.
  • To perform OCR and AI-based parsing only when you trigger those features.
  • To generate spending prediction, warnings, and AI insights based on your records.
  • To support stock search, quote refresh, and portfolio tracking.
  • To manage subscription status, restore purchases, and keep local preferences in sync with your experience.

4. Storage, retention, and deletion

Local storage

Some information is stored on your device, including preferences, cached data, login state, and local records.

Cloud sync

If you sign in, bookkeeping data, asset data, stock positions, and account-linked information may be stored through the configured backend.

Deletion

You can delete certain records inside the app. For cloud account deletion requests, please contact us using the email below.

Uninstalling the app may remove device-side data, but cloud-synced data can remain until it is deleted from the relevant account scope.

5. Your choices

  • You can delete individual records where the app supports this action.
  • You can sign out to clear local login state on the device.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of cloud-synced account data.

6. Children’s privacy

AI Wealth Ledger is not designed for children under 18, and we do not knowingly operate this bookkeeping flow for minors.
