Privacy Policy | AI Wealth Ledger

Updated: April 15, 2026 · Effective: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to AI Wealth Ledger. It explains what information the app may process, how the information is used, which third-party services may be involved, and how you can contact us about privacy or data deletion requests.

Data is only sent to relevant providers when you actively use a feature, such as receipt OCR, AI parsing, cloud sync, third-party sign-in, or subscription recovery.

1. Information the app may process

1. Information you provide or create

  • Accounting entries, including amount, type, category, date, and notes.
  • Asset and portfolio data, including account names, balances, symbols, quantities, and cost basis.
  • Custom categories and other user-created bookkeeping content.
  • Receipt images that you choose to upload for OCR.
  • Email address or third-party identity information when you sign in.

2. Local app data

  • Local sign-in state, language, region, base currency, and consent settings.
  • Cached stock search results, quote timestamps, and limited UI state.
  • Minimal diagnostic information needed for troubleshooting and stability.

2. Third-party services and purposes

3. How your information is used

  • To provide bookkeeping, asset tracking, and portfolio features.
  • To perform OCR, AI parsing, AI insights, and spending prediction when you trigger those features.
  • To support sign-in, cloud sync, and subscription recovery.
  • To remember your selected language, currency, and privacy preferences.
  • To improve reliability and diagnose technical issues.
We do not use your app data for unrelated advertising targeting or unrelated marketing purposes.

4. Storage and retention

  • Some information is stored locally on your device, including settings, cache, and local app state.
  • Signed-in account data may also be stored in the configured cloud backend for sync and recovery.
  • Uninstalling the app may remove local data from the device, but it does not automatically remove cloud-synced account data.

5. Your choices

  • You can delete individual records, assets, or positions where the app supports it.
  • You can sign out to clear local login state.
  • You can contact us if you want cloud account data deleted or if you have privacy-related questions.

6. Children’s privacy

AI Wealth Ledger is designed for general adult users. If you are under 18, please use the app only under guardian supervision or stop using the service.
